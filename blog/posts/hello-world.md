After years of building software for others, I finally got around to building something for myself — this website.

## Why rajbandi.dev?

I've spent 20+ years writing code across a wide range of domains — from enterprise .NET systems for Australian government departments to blockchain implementations and bioinformatics tools. Along the way, I've accumulated a lot of knowledge that lives in my head or scattered across private repos and Slack messages.

This blog is my attempt to change that.

## What to expect

I'll be writing about the things I work with and find interesting:

- **.NET and C#** — Tips, patterns, and lessons from real-world enterprise projects
- **Mobile development** — MAUI, Xamarin, Flutter, and cross-platform strategies
- **Blockchain** — My open-source implementations (ElasticSharp, LiskSharp, RiseSharp)
- **Bioinformatics & Health IT** — Where software meets science
- **DevOps** — Azure pipelines, CI/CD, and infrastructure as code
- **AI & tooling** — The new frontier that's changing how we build

## The stack

This site is intentionally simple:

- **Tailwind CSS** via CDN — utility-first styling, no build step
- **Vanilla JavaScript** — no frameworks, no dependencies
- **Markdown blog** — articles are plain `.md` files rendered client-side
- **GitHub Pages** — free, fast, and version-controlled

No webpack. No node_modules. No build pipeline. Just HTML, CSS, and JS that loads fast and gets out of the way.
